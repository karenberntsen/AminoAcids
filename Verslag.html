Figure 1: Log-scaled plot of the occurency of several combinations of chi1 and chi2 for isoleucine. All conformations in the database with a resolution a) higher than 2.0 Å b) lower than 3.0 Å are binned to bins of 1°. The scaling of both plots is different; they have another maximum value, which colour is set to white. These plots are devided into 9 regions, which represent the theoretically expected different clusters, numbered from 1 to 9.
